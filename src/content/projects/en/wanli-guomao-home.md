Wanli Trade Home is a learning-support site for the International Economics & Trade major at Zhejiang Wanli University. Using AI tools (TRAE, Codex), I took it from design to development to launch solo, gathering the info classmates use most into one clear entry point.

## What I did

- Built a multi-page responsive site in HTML / CSS / JavaScript, adapting to desktop, tablet and phone, with mobile navigation, smooth scrolling and QR-code popups.
- Organized the content around five resource categories for the major — overview, curriculum, academic competitions, certification guides and career paths — as a one-stop info hub.
- Hosted on GitHub and deployed via GitHub Pages, bound to the custom domain www.wlgmzj.cn, and iterated through 40+ commits.

## Why it matters

This project is where I combined my International Economics & Trade background with front-end development: rather than a flashy demo, I built something publicly accessible and maintainable from a real, everyday study need.
